Paris,France

Paris, often called "The City of Light," is the capital of France and one of the most iconic cities in the world. Known for its stunning architecture, world-renowned museums, and romantic atmosphere, Paris attracts millions of visitors each year. Landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum showcase the city's rich history and artistic heritage.

Beyond its beauty, Paris is also a global center for fashion, cuisine, and culture. The city is home to haute couture fashion houses, Michelin-starred restaurants, and vibrant neighborhoods like Montmartre and Le Marais.
